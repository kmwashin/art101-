Debugging

In our output, the numbers divisible by 7 weren't printing, only the word "boom." We fixed this by using jQuery code with 'append' to isolate the parts, print the “boom” line and specify the printing of the var num. It’s not the most practical or neatly formatted solution as it doesn't match the other multiple-factors in the function, but it was the best one that worked and was accessible given our working conditions and knowledge.
